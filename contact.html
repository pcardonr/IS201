    Ways that tracking Disneyland Resort wait times and refurnishment/closure schedules can be profitable

    1. Enthusiasts will pay for information
      • Massive fanbases of the Disney Parks and fans of IP in the parks will gladly pay for more information on their favorite attractions.

    2. Humans want to avoid being around eachother in large masses
      • People will pay for historical data that predicts future crowds if it can result in less people and lower wait times.

    3. Historical tracking creates a future advantage.
      • Being ahead of those who have not tracked and interpreted this data could provide for future monetary benefit.
